🌍 Policy Impact Simulator

Public policies can affect communities in very different ways. This tool helps identify potential benefits, concerns, and unintended consequences before policies are implemented.

Using AI-powered analysis, the system evaluates how groups such as low-income households, immigrants, disabled individuals, LGBTQ+ communities, students, seniors, and small business owners may experience a proposed policy.

About This Project

The Policy Impact Simulator is designed to support policymakers, researchers, advocates, and community leaders in understanding how policies may affect different populations.

Rather than predicting political opinions, the system focuses on identifying potential impacts, benefits, challenges, and equity considerations across multiple demographic groups.

The goal is to encourage more inclusive and informed decision-making by highlighting perspectives that may otherwise be overlooked.
